About Us

We care about your sweet tooth
We don’t judge we just satisfy.

My Story

Baking has always brought me peace and joy. What started as making treats for family and friends grew into something bigger
As people kept coming back for more. Turning that passion into a business wasn’t easy—it took late nights, trial and error, and faith.
But I believe good food brings people together, and through Baci Cakes, I get to share that love every day with custom cakes, cookies, and fresh, buttery loaves.

Meet The Team

Behind every cake, pastry, and loaf we bake is a passionate team of artisans who believe baking is more than a craft it’s an act of love.
Our team is made up of skilled bakers, decorators, and creatives who share a commitment to quality, creativity, and community.
From the early risers who prepare the day’s fresh dough, to the decorators who carefully craft custom designs,
each member of our team brings a unique touch that makes our bakery special.
We work together like family, combining traditional techniques with modern flair to create treats that are as beautiful as they are delicious.
Most importantly, we are united by a shared goal: to make every bite a moment of happiness for you.

Our Vision:Puting a smile on your face with our treats

At Baci Cakes, our vision is to bring joy, warmth, and unforgettable moments to every table through the art of baking.
We believe that every cake, pastry, and loaf should be more than just food. it should be an experience that tells a story of love, celebration, and togetherness.
We aspire to become a household name in baking by blending tradition with creativity, offering treats that are not only delicious but also crafted with the highest quality ingredients and care.
Whether it’s a simple loaf of bread, a custom-designed cake, or a tray of pastries, our goal is to make every bite a reason to smile. Our vision is to create a community of food lovers who trust us to sweeten their moments, from everyday indulgences to life’s biggest celebrations.
